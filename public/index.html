What's Your Story, Guys?

We're John and Mike, and we're brothers. We love roadtrips, tailgates, and pro football. This fall, we're hopping into an RV and criss-crossing the country to document these great American traditions. We've put together this video to tell you about the project.

Tweet Follow @tailgate_32

Let's Hit The Road!

We have a lot of work to do along the way. We're creating a mixed-media documentary covering road trip, tailgate, and football fan culture for all 32 teams in the NFL. As we travel across the country we'll interview fans, participate in local traditions, host a large public tailgate at each stadium, and uncover what makes these experiences special to each fan base. Along the way, we will release a series of fast-paced, high-quality video shorts showcasing each destination--highlighting as much local flavor as possible.

You can support Tailgate32 right now by picking up one of these slick t-shirts in your team's favorite colors from the Tailgate32 store.

We look forward to hanging out with you when we pass through your city this fall!
