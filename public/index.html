This fall Mike and John will be criss crossing the country in an RV and throwing a tailgate party at a home game for each of the 32 football teams. We'll be documenting in real-time our experiences on the road, our engagement with the fans in each city, the tailgates and the games themselves. We'd love for you to join as at a tailgate when we come to your city!

We're currently raising $5,000 to get this project off the ground. If you donate $25 to our Kickstarter campaign we'll send you this cool Tailgate32 shirt customized in your favorite team's colors!
