Let's Hit The Road!

Hi. We're John and Mike. We're brothers. We love roadtrips, tailgates, and pro football.


View Larger

This fall, we're hopping into an RV and criss-crossing the country to document everything we can about these great American traditions. We'll throw a large public tailgate at a home game of each NFL team and invite you to join us at every stop along the way. We want you to share your stories, traditions, and enthusiasm with us-- with photos, videos, interviews, and across social media. Ultimately, we'll compile the definitive story of the pro football tailgating experience and share it with everyone.

We're currently raising $6,000 to get this project off the ground. If you're able to donate $25 to our Kickstarter campaign, we'd love to send you this cool Tailgate32 shirt customized in your favorite team's colors!

We look forward to hanging out with you when we pass through your city this fall!

What Have We Been Up To?


Wednesday, May 9th

Aidan came by to do another video shoot. This time he used a camera that specializes in shots with a very shallow depth of field. We're expecting the "fine cut" from him tomorrow evening. Looks like we're on target for a mid-week launch next week.

Monday, May 7th

We hassled all of our friends trying to dig up another 30-40 photos that we can use to finish up our promo video. Then we headed out to Joe's on Weed St. to meet the Dirty River Boys and watch their show. They've generously offered us rights to their song "Raise Some Hell Tonight" which will be featured in the video. We're excited about the opportunity to connect with more bands like the DRB's over the course of our trip-- it seems like a great avenue for cross-promotion for both the bands and Tailgate32.
