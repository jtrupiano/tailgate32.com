What's Your Story, Guys?

We're John and Mike and we're brothers. We love roadtrips, tailgates, and pro football. This fall, we're hopping into an RV and criss-crossing the country to document these great American traditions. We've put together this video to tell you about the project.

Tweet Follow @tailgate_32

Let's Hit The Road!

We have a lot of work to do along the way. We're throwing a large public tailgate at a home game of each NFL team and inviting you to join us at every stop. We want to share your stories, traditions, and enthusiasm through photos, videos, interviews, and across social media. Ultimately, we'll compile the definitive story of the pro football tailgating experience and share it with everyone.

We are currently raising $6,000 to get this project off the ground. If you're able to donate $25 to our Kickstarter campaign, we'd love to print off this slick Tailgate32 shirt customized in your favorite team's colors for you!

We look forward to hanging out with you when we pass through your city this fall!
