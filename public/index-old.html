Video Artifacts!

Thursday, April 26 at 4:45PM by John

A lot of friends contributed tailgate photos for us to include in our Kickstarter video. We also conducted three phone interviews gathering stories from football fans about their tailgating experiences. Our initial intent was to simply gather sound bites that could be used in the video. But we've recorded some really good stories of specific tailgating experiences that could be useful for some other production down the line. We plan to continue to record as many interviews as we can over the next couple of months, so if you're interested in sharing your story please reach out to us!

We also made a little more headway on the website, but I'm not entirely happy with the direction I'm going in. We're considering getting some help from a friend who's a little more talented at design.
